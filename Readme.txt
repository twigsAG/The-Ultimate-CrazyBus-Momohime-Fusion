The Ultimate CrazyBus is a compilation of the best Venezuelan bus simulators, featuring the original CrazyBus and my very own CrazyBus: Tucson to Las Vegas. 

The Original CrazyBus is almost 1:1 with the original v2.00r30 (Momohime) version, and is the most realistic simulation of a Venezuelan bus.

CrazyBus: Tucson to Las Vegas is an idea I had for quite a while. It took only a few days to figure out how to make it work.
Anyways, this is just CrazyBus and Desert Bus combined. You have to drive for 8 hours and then you win!

Controls:
= These mimic a Sega Genesis 3-button Control Pad =
<- -> - Move
Z - Honk the horn, Action button
X - Turn on the Radio, Turn off the Radio, Back to menu button
C - Back to Title (OG CrazyBus), change Radio song (TtLV)
Enter - Back to Title (TtLV), change Radio song (OG CrazyBus)
Escape - Back to Main Menu

Disclaimer: Do not turn on the "Legacy built-in songs" cheat if you want to stream the game, as many of the legacy radio songs are either copyrighted and/or NSFW.
